About Us

TalentSync HR Solutions is a professional recruitment and staffing agency committed to delivering tailored hiring solutions for businesses and meaningful career opportunities for job seekers. Based in Pune, Maharashtra, we are a registered MSME company operating since March 2023.

Who We Are

We specialize in talent acquisition across various sectors including BPO, Finance, Banking & Insurance, IT/ITES, Retail, FMCG, and the Social Sector. Our approach is candidate-focused and employer-driven, ensuring a perfect sync between skills and opportunities.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between talent and industry by offering high-quality, customized hiring support, career development services, and a trusted partnership model with businesses.
